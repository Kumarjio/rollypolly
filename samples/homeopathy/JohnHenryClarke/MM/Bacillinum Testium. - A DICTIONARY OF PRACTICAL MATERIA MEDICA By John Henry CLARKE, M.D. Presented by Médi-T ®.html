Bacillinum Testium.

A nosode prepared from tuberculous testicle.

Clinical.─Inguinal glands, disease of. Mesenteric glands, disease of. Phthisis. Testicles, tubercle of.

Characteristics.─This preparation has been used by Burnett as having a more direct relation to the lower half of the body than the pulmonary Bacillinum. My own experience confirms the correctness of this inference; but it must not be supposed that Bac. test. does not act in pulmonary cases, or vice versâ.
