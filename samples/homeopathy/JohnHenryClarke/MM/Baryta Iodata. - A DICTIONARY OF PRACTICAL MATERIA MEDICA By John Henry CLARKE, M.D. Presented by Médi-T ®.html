Baryta Iodata.

Baric iodide. Ba I2. Trituration.

Clinical.─Breast, cancer of. Cancer. Glands, enlargement of. Tumours.

Characteristics.─Baryt. iod. has been used empirically, especially in cases of glandular enlargement and new growths, on indications suggested by its two elements.
