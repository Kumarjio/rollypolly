ACUTE SYNOVITIS.
In rheumatic subjects, fever, restlessness, anxiety, pain, Aco. 3, 1h.
Pain < with every movement, part sensitive to touch, Bry. 3, 1h.
In women and children, pain < by heat > by cold, Puls. 3, 1h.
Where there is much swelling and little pain, Apis 3x, 2h.
When suppuration has taken place, Hep. 6, 4h. (externally, a lotion of Hep. 6, a teaspoonful to a wineglassful of distilled water).
Where discharge has commenced, Sil. 6, 4h. (externally, a lotion of Sil. 6, a teaspoonful to a wine glassful of distilled water).
