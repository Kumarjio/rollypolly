DEDICATION
To all physicians of all schools of medicine who are honestly open to conviction as to the truth of 'Similia Similibus Curantur'.

NOTE

     At the risk of being charged with egotism I have given many more of my own cases than any one else. My reason for so doing is that this collection of cases is a sort of companion piece of my "Leaders in Homoeopathic Therapeuticcs," and as such would naturally be expected to give my own experience in the clinical application of the remedies treated, and coroborated by some experiences of many others entitled to testimony.

     Let me here earnestly request that my readers do not fail to read the preface. It will explain why more remedies, just as good, were not incorporated into this work.

E. B. Nash
